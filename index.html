Hangman

Input a letter of the alphabet or a word into the text box and press guess.

If the letter is contained in the answer, the letter will be displayed on the page.

If you guess all the correct letters or quess the answer you will win the game!

If you guess a letter not contained in the answer or the wrong answer a portion of the hangman will be added.

If all the portions of the hangman are added or you run out of guessess you will lose the game
